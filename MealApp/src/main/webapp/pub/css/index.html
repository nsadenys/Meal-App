About us

This is Meal App website and we deliver food

With the help of Meal App website, restaurants can sell or share their leftovers to those in need. Neighbors can also offer food they don’t need or want to others for free. And volunteers can deliver meals to people who struggle to find adequate nutritious food. Meal App provides an interface between restaurant owners, neighbors and volunteers involved in distributing food from restaurants, neighbors and will help prevent wastages food and to feed the people in need such as homeless and poor people. A donor(neighbor) can register on the app and generate a request to pick up the leftover food. A notification on it then goes to the volunteers of the neighbors who can distribute food..
